SCHOLARSHIP


An amount of money given by a school, college, university, or other organization to pay for the studies of a person with great ability but little money.


HOW TO GET A SCHOLARSHIP


The best way to begin seeking scholarships is to create a self awareness and do your research.Every college and university has a bounty of scholarships to be given to the meritorious students.Usually they will be listed on the schools or college's website


DIFFERENT TYPES OF SCHOLARSHIPS



  1. Merit Scholarships
  2. Need based scholarships
  3. Athletic Scholarships
  4. Full / Partial scholarships
  5. Fellowships
  6. Major-specific scholarships
  7. Ethnic/Majority and Religious scholarships


ADVANTAGES OF SCHOLARSHIPS


  1. Getting a scholarship is to have free money from the door to pursue further education or to meet other financial needs.
  2. Students can completely concentrate on their studies without having any type of financial burden on them like paying the fees ,expenses on books and other study materials etc..
  3. The tag of 'high prestige' is directly linked to an award of scholarship,as scholarships are awarded to students who hold a proven track record of brilliant score a nd excellence in their respective fields
  4. When applying for a job scholrships serve as a highlight on a person's job resume as there are also brilliant resume boosters and look fantastic when listed on the resume
  5. Scholarships are far better than loans
